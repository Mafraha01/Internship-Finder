About us

Introduction

We are a group of Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology (BICT) students from the University of Kelaniya. Our team came together to develop the Intern Finder application as part of an assignment for our course.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to simplify the process of connecting students with internship opportunities, helping them kickstart their careers. We envision a future where every student has access to valuable internship experiences.

Objectives

  • To create a user-friendly platform for students to discover internship opportunities.
  • To provide employers with a streamlined process for posting internship positions.
  • To bridge the gap between students and employers in the internship search process.

Project Description

Intern Finder is a web application designed to connect students seeking internships with employers offering internship opportunities. Through our platform, students can browse and apply for internships in various industries, while employers can post internship positions and manage applicants.

Features

  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation.
  • Comprehensive job listings with detailed descriptions.
  • Customizable search filters to find relevant internships.
  • Secure applicant tracking system for employers.
  • Notifications for application status updates.

Development Process

The Intern Finder application was developed using Django, a high-level Python web framework. Our team followed an iterative development process, incorporating feedback from users and stakeholders to improve the application's functionality and usability.

Acknowledgments

We would like to thank our course instructor for providing guidance and support throughout the development process. We are also grateful to our fellow classmates who contributed their ideas and feedback to make this project a success.

Future Plans

In the future, we plan to enhance the Intern Finder application by adding additional features such as a messaging system for communication between students and employers, and a rating system for internship experiences.
